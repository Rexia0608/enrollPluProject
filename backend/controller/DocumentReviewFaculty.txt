VISION
Laguna Science and Technology College envisions itself as a
growing institution of learning and developing individuals
technically, technologically and professionally thus becoming useful
citizens of the nation contributing to its greatness.  

MISSION
The mission of Laguna Science and Technology College is to
train young men and women to become productive citizens fully
equipped with rightful skills and values that the modern industries
demand. 





OBJECTIVES
. Provide individuals different opportunities to become God-fearing, self-disciplined and skilled.

. Join hands with the government and private entities in meeting the current and future needs of the labor market.

. Intensify linkages in providing employment to the current and future graduates. 

. Get involved and realize the value and importance of becoming entrepreneurs with the aid of technical and vocational courses being offered.
